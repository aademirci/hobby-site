About this diary

I have been enjoying a barefoot lifestyle for a decade and I take a snapshot of the moment by adding entries to this diary. These entries are made of 3 essential things:

  • a top-down photo which documents that I am really barefoot
  • a photo of what I see in front of me
  • and a descriptive text for the moment

Adding entries to this diary creates a collection of happy moments for me to return at any time because I feel happy and free when I just be barefoot outside, no matter whether I am walking or running. Yes, I ran a few 10k's without shoes.

There are of course times that I cannot be simply barefoot, so I switched my footwear closet completely to the minimalist shoes and sandals. These have thin and flexible soles with zero drop from heel to toe, and each has a wide toe box as well.
